Databases

I teach multiple flavours of this module: databases and interfaces is the undergraduate module, while databases, interfaces and software design principles is the postgraduate version of the module. While they differ in a few points (the postgraduate module is wider and touches on software design and requirement analysis), they are very similar in scope.

Human-AI Interaction

This module is part of the Human-Computer Interaction theme, and the Artificial Intelligence and Optimisation theme in the School of Computer Science. It is an introduction to the design of human-AI interaction to ensure the AI-driven systems we built are beneficial and useful to people.

Computer Graphics

This module focuses on teaching the principles of computer graphics. Being an introduction to computer graphics, this module demonstrates the benefits of linking theory and practice.
